Eloquence contest June 17, 2022

Bienvenue

We invite you to our eloquence contest

We have decided to organize a speech contest on June 17, 2022

On June 17, 2022 we are organizing an eloquence contest for the first time.

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to experience this unique moment with us

The ambition of the eloquence days is to honor public speaking.

Initially the day will begin with a contest of eloquence which you will be able to attend freely on the great stakes of our current world.
followed by a prize giving for the best of them

We then hope to see you continue the day with us in the afternoon with initiation workshops in public speaking. Teachers of communication in the workplace are waiting for you for this initiation of public discourse in an interactive and fun way.

Finally, we will end this wonderful day with public speaking professionals to answer all your questions
All in a whirlwind of good humor.

Please confirm your presence by June 14 at the latest in the form made available on this same site.
